Pipfrosch Press Taxonomy Notes

Note: This section does not appear in the original article.

Arvicola riparius [Ord, 1825] is now considered to be a junior synonym of Microtus pennsylvanicus [Ord, 1815]. Several subspecies are described, Illinois falls within the range of the nominate subspecies M. p. pennsylvanicus [Ord, 1815] although many no longer recognize the validity of any subspecies within M. pennsylvanicus.

This species is currently referred to using the common name ‘Meadow Vole’.
