  • Obtain quality photographs as specified in the Needed Photographs appendix section that follows.

Unfortunately, I am a long ways away from where many of the animals described in this paper are found, and my own photographs frequently do not meet my quality expectations. I must concede that many high school students with an artistic eye and no formal photography training take better photographs than I do.
