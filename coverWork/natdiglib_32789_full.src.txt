Thirteen-lined ground squirrel
Ictidomys tridecemlineatus
nctcimages@fws.gov
Smith, Grayson, USFWS

Thirteen-lined ground squirrels are diurnal and most active at midday and on warm sunny days. They dig shallow blind-end emergency burrows as well as complex deeper underground burrows for nesting and hibernation.
